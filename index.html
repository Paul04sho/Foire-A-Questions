FAQ

What's web development ?

Web development is the process of creating websites and web applications. It involves a combination of programming, design, and content management to build functional and visually appealing online platforms.

How many languages do i need ?

To start web development, you typically need to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These are the foundational languages for building web pages. As you progress, you may also explore server-side languages like Python, PHP, or Ruby, and frameworks like React or Angular.

Can i learn it in 6 months ?

Yes, it is possible to learn the basics of web development in 6 months with consistent effort and practice. Many people start with online courses, tutorials, and projects to build their skills. However, becoming proficient may take longer depending on your learning pace and the complexity of the projects you undertake.
